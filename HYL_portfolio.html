About Me

I am a research assistant in Academia Sinica, advised by Jen-Chun Lin and Hong-Yuan Mark Liao. I received my B.S degree in Computer Science and Information Engineering at National Central University. I used to be a machine learning engineer at Industrial Technology Research Institute and Inventec Appliances Corp. My research interest is to enable cross-modal generation to produce art pieces with the creativity and quality as human works. If you would like to know more about me, please contact me via email.
